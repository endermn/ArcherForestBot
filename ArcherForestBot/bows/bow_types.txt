Bow Types:

Stone Bow = +X Produced Arrow Level (Equip on Bow's specific archer and use for Hermes team)
Wooden Bow = +X Archer Own Arrow's Level (Best on Hedwig, Kawiil (if >lv4))
Steel Bow = +X Archer Own Level (Best on Kawiil (if <lv4), Taurus, and also great for any archers with useful passives/abilities)
