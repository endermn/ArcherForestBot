Bow Grade:

Wooden Bows have higher min/max stats than Steel and Stone Bows. Steel and Stone Bows have the same min/max stats 
Wooden Bows:
1 star: +1-6/+1-2
2 star: +6-12/+2-4
3 star: +12-18/+4-6
4 star: +18-24/+6-8
5 star: +24-30/+8-10

Stone/Steel Bows:
1 star: +1-4/+1-2
2 star: +4-8/+2-3
3 star: +8-12/+3-4
4 star: +12-16/+4-5
5 star: +16-20/+5-6
